Key Assumptions — TP3
  • 1M signups: 100% web traffic exposure, Mobile App launch Aug-26, $20M paid media at $200 CPA. Jul-15 update: Digital web underperforming — scaling to $30M (+$10M at $300 CPA).
  • Monetization: 18% gross / 10% net Free-to-Paid rate. 7% cannibalization baked in. SKU mix 50% Low End ($15/mo) / 50% Core ($95/mo).
  • In-year retention: Core 85%, Low end 65% → 57K net paid subs from 75K GNS adds. Payments GNA: 34K at 3.4% attach rate → 8.5K active merchants at year-end → $2M revenue.
  • Retention saves: 105K at-risk subs offered Free downgrade → 9K re-subscribe to paid (8.5% reactivation).
  • Jul-15 risk: Digital GNS reduced 50K → 18K (−32K) due to web test underperformance. Net revised target: 57K GNS — leaving an ~18K gap vs 75K plan. Low-confidence bets account for 15K of gap fill.
